Welcome to Animal Suvidha





Animal Suvidha - A help for innocents

We are living in a rapid-moving world, where an injured person hardly has a chance to get help. And that too, when people know whom to call. In such a scenario, if it were an animal, people generally have no idea whom to call and how to help, which furthermore increases the chances of that animal dying. Hence, with this project, we have a vision to make the process of helping animals, frictionless. With this, we would be able to save lives of countless animal. In the future, we would make the application easier by detecting the type of animal and its injury simply by showing the camera towards our injured animal. This would hence make the process effortless, so that more and more people are willing to help the injured animal.
